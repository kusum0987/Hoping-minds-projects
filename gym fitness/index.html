what is a body weight workout

A bodyweight workout is a type of exercise routine that relies solely on the individual's own body weight for resistance, rather than using external equipment or weights. These workouts can be performed virtually anywhere and are suitable for various fitness levels. Bodyweight exercises are effective for building strength, flexibility, and endurance. Some common bodyweight exercises include:
Push-ups: Targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
Pull-ups/Chin-ups: Engages the muscles in the back and arms.
Squats: Works the muscles in the legs, particularly the quadriceps and glutes.
Lunges: Targets the legs and helps improve balance.
Planks: Engages the core muscles.

BUILDER

Becoming a bodybuilder involves a combination of consistent and targeted strength training, proper nutrition, adequate rest, and dedication to the lifestyle. Here are some key aspects to consider if you're interested in pursuing bodybuilding Strength Training:
Progressive Resistance Training: Gradually increase the resistance or weight you lift over time to stimulate muscle growth.
Split Training: Divide your workout routine to focus on specific muscle groups on different days (e.g., chest and triceps, back and biceps, legs).
Caloric Surplus: To build muscle, you generally need to consume more calories than your body burns (caloric surplus).
Adequate Sleep: Ensure you get enough quality sleep to support recovery and muscle growth.
Regular Workouts: Consistency is key in bodybuilding. Stick to your training schedule and be patient with the results.
Commitment: Bodybuilding requires dedication and commitment. Be prepared for both physical and mental challenges.
Goal Setting: Set realistic short-term and long-term goals to stay motivated and track your progress.:
